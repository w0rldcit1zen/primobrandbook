Build rules — for every asset above

ElementRule
Margins89px on 1080-wide canvases (Fibonacci); 80px on 9:16 and 1:1 shown for platform-UI clearance.
Focal anchorGolden section of canvas height: 4:5 → y=516/834 · 9:16 → y=734/1187 · 1:1 → y=413/667.
On-image typeFibonacci only. Hooks 89–110px, statements 55–76px, support 34–38px. Never below 34px on a feed asset; 13px does not exist on social.
AccentBright Signal Green on Indigo/Slate; Signal Green Deep on Paper/Parchment. One accent phrase per asset.
ChartsFive-series ramp maximum, direct labels, source line — meaning never in colour alone.
Footer stripLockup left + PRIMO.COMMUNICATION right, inside margins; compact lockup under 180px width.
Safe areas (9:16)Keep type out of top 180px and bottom 220px — platform UI overlays live there.
Alt textAlways, every platform. The on-image message must also appear in the caption.

Voice on social

Thesis first; numbers, not adjectives; one idea per asset. On-image text is declarative and short — the caption can flow. Never the construction "It's not just X — it's Y." No hype words, no exclamation marks.
