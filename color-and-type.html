03 · COLOUR & TYPE

A green that means go.

Signal Green is the brand in one colour. A signal is what communication is — something sent, received, understood. And green is the light that says move: ready to go, cleared to proceed. It carries movement and readiness in a field where every competitor — the big consultancies, the UN system itself — is blue. Around it: the deep calm of Indigo, the warmth of Parchment (the colour of paper, letters, stories kept), and the quiet grays that let the signal be heard. Chosen for meaning; proven by measurement.

Deep Indigo · #1A1B2EThe base. Institutional weight without the glare of pure black — the room at night where the stories get told.
Slate · #2C2F3FRaised surface — cards and panels on Indigo.
Signal Green · #12B981The signal itself, on DARK backgrounds. Movement, readiness, go. Measured 6.68:1 on Indigo — clears AA for body text.
Signal Green Deep · #0A6B4BThe same signal for LIGHT backgrounds — mandatory there. Measured 6.53:1 on white.
Parchment · #EFE1CCThe warm surface — paper, letters, the family table. Warmth as a place to read, never as an accent.
Mist Gray · #B1B1B1Metadata and secondary text on dark.
Paper White · #FFFFFFType on dark; light surface.

The rule that is easiest to break

ON DARK

Use Signal Green #12B981 — measured 6.68:1.

ON LIGHT (WHITE / PARCHMENT)

Use Signal Green Deep #0A6B4B — measured 6.53:1. Bright Signal Green on white is 2.54:1 — a signal nobody can read.

TYPE — ONE SEQUENCE, EVERY SIZE

Montserrat 800 for display and headings; Roboto for text. Every size comes from one sequence — 13 · 21 · 34 · 55 · 89 · 144 — so every relationship is traceable and no decision is arbitrary. Discipline you can see.

Aa 89 H1 · line-height 1.1
Aa 55 H2 · 1.15
Aa 34 H3 · 1.25
Aa 21 — body is 21px on 34px leading (1.619): comfortable to read, compliant by construction. Body · Roboto 400
Aa 13 — labels, captions, metadata only. Never body copy. Caption · 1.615

Measure: 66ch maximum line length. Display 144px reserved for hero moments. Spacing uses the same sequence in px. Focal elements land on the golden section (0.382 / 0.618) — one consistent, traceable ratio; no claims of cosmic beauty.
