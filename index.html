THE PRIMO BRAND BOOK · V2.0

Communication is not a given. It is a craft.

Primo was founded on that lesson — learned as a student of how power communicates, and proven over fifteen years practicing global communication for policy making inside the UN system. Institutions assume they will be understood. They won't be. Being understood is earned, message by message, and that earning is a skill, an expertise, a discipline. Primo exists to bring it.

01 · THE NAME

Primo means the best of something.
It also means cousin.

In Spanish, primo carries both meanings at once — of the highest quality, and family. The legend in our founder's family is that it was his first word. As legends go, there is some truth in it: if not the first, one of the first — a kid in a big Mexican-American family in Los Angeles greeting everyone he knew as primo.

What that child was actually watching was a communication network. A large Latin American family runs on shared information — word of mouth, telephone calls, parties, get-togethers. Stories told and retold around the table: the funny ones, the sad ones, everything life brings. News travelled, meaning was made, and everyone was brought along. That was the first newsroom, the first strategy office, the first masterclass in narrative.

That is what Primo brings to strategic communication: the highest standard of the craft, carried with the instinct of the family table — stories that travel, told so that everyone is brought along.

02 · WHAT WE CARRY

Roots and signals.

Storytelling first

Growing up bilingual, first in the family to go to college, then to work abroad — the complexity of that road taught one thing: when the work gets complex, you go back to your roots. You tell the story. Everything else serves it.

Craft, earned

Fifteen years across WMO, UNICEF, FSC, and the ITU — the discipline of institutional communication learned where the stakes are measured in trust, mandates, and lives.

Digital at the heart — the signal

Every format, every channel, and now the AI-native way of working: agentic pipelines, answer engines, systems that scale the work. The systems multiply the story; they never replace it. Fundamentals stay at the heart.

For everyone

A story only counts if it is received. So the brand is built to be received by everyone — colour, type, and motion measured against WCAG 2.2 AA, the same standard our institutional deliverables are procured against. Method in service of a value.
