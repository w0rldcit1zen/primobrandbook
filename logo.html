The Signal mark.

Two triangles — two ends of communication — meeting in the middle. A speaker and a listener. A sender and a receiver. An institution and its public. Where they touch is where meaning happens, and that meeting point sits at the centre of the wordmark, doing the work of the M in PRIMO. Around the studio we call it the bowtie; its real name is what it does.

On dark — the workhorse. White wordmark, Signal Green mark. For Indigo and Slate surfaces.

On light. Indigo wordmark, Signal Green mark. For Paper and Parchment.

Mark alone. App icons and avatars. Minimum 24px.

Rules

RuleValue
Clear spaceThe height of the Signal mark, on all sides. The meeting point needs room to be seen.
Minimum width — primary lockup180px. Below this the descriptor is illegible — switch to compact.
Minimum width — compact90px.
Minimum size — mark alone24px.
ContrastLogos are exempt under WCAG 1.4.3 (logotypes excluded). Every SVG nonetheless ships with <title> and aria-label.

Misuse

  • Do not recolour the mark — it is Signal Green in every variant.
  • Do not set the wordmark in bright Signal Green on light surfaces.
  • Do not stretch, outline, shadow, or rotate — the two ends must meet cleanly.
  • Do not place on busy photography without a solid Indigo or Paper field.
